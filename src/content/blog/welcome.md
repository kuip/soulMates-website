We're excited to introduce **soulMates**: a new way to discover who you are through music, with your friends and find people who truly share your taste.

## How It Works

soulMates analyzes your music preferences across 12 musical axes to build a unique **music genome**. This is your musical fingerprint. As you swipe through tracks, the app learns what moves you and maps it to 30 base personality traits that compose the Big Five and the 16 personalities from MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator).

## Find Your People

Once your music DNA takes shape, soulMates matches you with people whose musical aura aligns with yours. Share tracks with your matches, and discover music together. Invite your friends and listen to music together, in sync, and share emoji reactions.

## What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional music apps, soulMates isn't just about *what* you listen to, it's about *why* you listen to it. Two people might love completely different artists but share the same underlying musical personality. That's where real connection happens.
